- ## Contributor
	- Contributors work to fulfill #[superbenefit](archive/clarity/Tags/superbenefit.md)'s #[purpose](data/concepts/purpose.md) by completing #[tasks](data/concepts/tasks.md) and collaborating on #[index](archive/clarity/Projects/index.md) to fulfill #[goals](data/concepts/goals.md).
	- ### Tiered Privileges
		- Contributors gain privileges and permissions by earning #[$sprb](archive/clarity/Tags/$sprb.md).
		- ### Tier1
			- **Requirements:**
				- 100 $SPRB or SB NFT
			- **Privileges: **
				- T1 Discord Permissions, T1 Clarity Permissions
				- Can earn [bounties](archive/clarity/Tags/bounties.md) and request [tasks](data/concepts/tasks.md)
		- ### Tier 2
			- **Requirements:**
				- 1000 $SPRB, Contributor Opt-In
			- **Privileges: **
				- T2 Discord Permissions, T2 Clarity Permissions
				- Can create & claim [tasks](data/concepts/tasks.md)
		- ### Tier 3
			- **Requirements:**
				- 10000 $SPRB, Rep?
			- **Privileges: **
				- T2 Discord Permissions, T2 Clarity Permissions
				- Can create [proposals](archive/clarity/Tags/proposals.md) & [index](archive/clarity/Projects/index.md)
