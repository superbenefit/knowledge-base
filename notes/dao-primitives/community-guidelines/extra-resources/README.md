Four one-page guides for navigating conflict in peer-to-peer community contexts. Use these alongside your Conflict Response Plan.

## The Guides

1. **[Guide 1: Navigating Conflict Recognition and Discourse](notes/dao-primitives/community-guidelines/extra-resources/guide-1-discourse.md)**  
   For community members addressing commitment breaches directly

2. **[Guide 2: Mediating Conflict](notes/dao-primitives/community-guidelines/extra-resources/guide-2-mediation.md)**  
   For community members facilitating when direct discourse hasn't worked

3. **[Guide 3: Arbitrating Conflict](notes/dao-primitives/community-guidelines/extra-resources/guide-3-arbitration.md)**  
   For community members making binding decisions when mediation fails

4. **[Guide 4: Participating in Mediation and Arbitration](notes/dao-primitives/community-guidelines/extra-resources/guide-4-participation.md)**  
   For community members whose conflict has moved beyond direct discourse
