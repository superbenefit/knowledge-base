In Web3 [governance](data/concepts/governance.md), delegation refers to the process where token holders transfer their voting [power](data/concepts/power.md) or [decision-making](archive/clarity/Tags/decision-making.md) authority to a trusted representative, allowing more efficient [participation](participation.md) in [decentralized](data/concepts/decentralization.md) decision-making processes.
