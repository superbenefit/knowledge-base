## Discourse & Communication

**We Have to Talk: Checklist for Difficult Conversations**  
Preparation framework with specific conversation openers.  
https://www.judyringer.com/resources/articles/we-have-to-talk-a-stepbystep-checklist-for-difficult-conversations.php

**Four Parts of Accountability** (Mia Mingus)  
How to give genuine apologies with specific language examples.  
https://leavingevidence.wordpress.com/2019/12/18/how-to-give-a-good-apology-part-1-the-four-parts-of-accountability/

**Calling In: A Toolkit**  
Comparison of calling in vs. calling out with sample statements.  
https://hsc.unm.edu/medicine/education/leo/_media/quarterly-reports/leo-toolkit_calling-in-vs-calling-out.pdf

**Boundary Setting Toolkit**  
Comprehensive guide with worksheets for all relationship types.  
https://care.ucdavis.edu/sites/g/files/dgvnsk2951/files/inline-files/Boundary%20Setting%20Toolkit%20(2).pdf
